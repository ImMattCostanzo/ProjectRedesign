More About St John

St John is one of the Virgin Islands in the Caribbean Sea and a constituent district of the United States Virgin Islands, an unincorporated territory of the United States. The island is home to many amazing creatures from dolphins, turtles and rays to wild donkeys from the islands sugar plantation days.
