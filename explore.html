Attractions of St John


St John has many attractions to explore. From beaches to trails to local cuisine, here are some of the best ones we have chosen to make your adventure unforgettable. You can explore the history and culture of the island. These activities will surely keep you busy and help you enjoy your stay on St. John. These selections will be available to everyone of all ages.

Reef Bay Trail


As part of the V.I. National Park, the Reef Bay Trail is a well maintained 4.4 mile hike. You may choose to go the trail on your own or with a NPS tour guide. Along the forest you will see old sugar mills, 2000 year old petroglyphs and more. Be on the lookout for island wildlife. Make sure you bring your water and a camera!

Trunk Bay Beach


As one of the most popular spots on St John, Trunk Bay is a true caribbean gem. With its crystal clear water, white sand, and underwater snorkeling trail; it has been voted as one of the top 10 beaches in the world. You can grab a bite to eat, relax under the shade of a sea grape tree, or explore the island sea life on the guided snorkeling trail. Whatever you choose, you too will agree that indeed Trunk Bay is a mandatory stop on your visit to St John.

Lime Out


Located on the island's east end is the Lime Out; a taco bar floating in the Coral Bay Harbor, only accessable by boat. They offer local cocktails, drinks and tacos with a caribbean twist. Come out and enjoy the sun, sea and a saste of St John. This unique experience is one you will remember forever.
